# Awesome Chocolates Commercial Analysis: Targeting Profitability in Key Markets

## 1. Project Background

Awesome Chocolates, a global confectionery company, requires a comprehensive commercial performance review to drive profitability and benchmark sales team efficiency. The primary challenge is the severe disparity in **Profit LBS% (Profitability Percentage)** across global regions, particularly the low performance in the India market.

This project delivers a deep-dive into sales, cost, and profit data to inform decisions on product line pruning, sales team incentive structures, and crucial market restructuring.

Insights and recommendations are provided on the following key areas:
* **Regional Profitability Crisis:** Evaluation of the critical low-margin performance in the India market.
* **Product Line Pruning:** Identification of low-LBS% products globally and within problem markets.
* **Sales Team Benchmarking:** Comparison of Sales vs. Profit generated by individual team members.

## 3. Executive Summary

Awesome Chocolates generated **$34M in total sales** and **$21M in profit**, but performance varies dramatically by region. The **India market** exhibits critical issues, including a near-stagnant **2.8% MOM growth** and an extremely low **3.8% Profit LBS%**. This indicates severe cost-to-sale inefficiencies in that market. The strategic focus must immediately shift to diagnosing the India market's cost drivers and pruning low-margin products globally.

## 4. Business Recommendations

Based on the detailed commercial performance analysis, the following actions are critical to stabilize sales, reduce cost-to-sale ratios, and maximize profitability (Profit LBS%):

* **1. Emergency Review of India Market:** Launch an immediate investigation into the high operational costs or severe discounting contributing to the **3.8% Profit LBS% in India**. Strategic intervention, potentially including restructuring local contracts or logistics, is required.
* **2. Global Product Line Pruning:** Strategically review the low-margin products, specifically **50% Dark Bites** and **Milk Bars**. The recommendation is to either **reformulate** the cost structure or **discontinue** these products to immediately boost the overall company profitability percentage.
* **3. Optimize Shipment Logistics:** Address the high volume of **small shipments** (as shown in the Shipment Analysis). Implement minimum order quantities or use aggregated freight options to reduce logistical costs.
* **4. Leverage Top Sales Performers:** Analyze the sales strategies of high-LBS% earners like **Gunnar Cockshoot**. Formalize their methods and implement mandatory training for the entire sales force.
* **5. Strategic Product Focus in India:** Shift the product focus in India away from low-margin items like **Milk Bars** and **Raspberry Choco** toward products with proven global LBS% success.
